Questions

Fair questions, straight answers.

Do my customers see a different phone number?

No. Customers call the same number they always have. The text-back comes from a dedicated local number for your business, and every message identifies your business by name.

What if I answer the call?

Then nothing happens — we only step in when a call goes unanswered. You keep working exactly the way you do now.

Can I turn it off or cancel?

Anytime. Call forwarding is switched off with one dial code on your phone, and there's no contract holding you.

Is this compliant with texting rules?

Yes. Messages are only sent to people who contact your business or your existing customers who agreed to hear from you. Every message includes opt-out instructions, and STOP requests are honored immediately.
